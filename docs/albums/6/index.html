Podda Ponti Architetti

This was a very special project. I was asked to create a website that would be a personal portfolio for two architects, husband and wife, two persons with elegant and precise tastes. To make the most out of photographic images, I created a single-page website, with a strong visual identity/typography and a strong user-interaction. I used Koken, a new generation CMS, for the management of the photographic contents, and Isotope, a client-side JavaScript library, to carry out the dynamic effects in the page grid. Website: www.poddapontiarchitetti.it
