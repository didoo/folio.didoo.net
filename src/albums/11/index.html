ForGreen

In the beginning of 2012, I was hired to do a complete restyling of the ForGreen website, made by me some years before, to conform to the new brand identity and the changed company mission. I took care of the entire project, from the concept to the visual design, from the creation/revision of contents (text and photos) to the definition of the information architecture, from the development of the front-end (HTML5/CSS3) to that of the back-end (a custom-made CMS, developed using Codeigniter, a PHP framework, and Twitter Bootrap, a web-framework).The narrative theme of the website is a storytelling in vertical blocks, as "tone of voice" in their corporate presentation. Website: www.forgreen.it
