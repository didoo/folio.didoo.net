Il Foglio Quotidiano

In February 2008, I proceeded to the complete redesign of the public website and the building of the custom-made CMS used for publishing and managing content. In October of the same year, it was introduced on the website a paywall for subscribers, the first in Italy for a national newspaper. In September 2010, it was launched the iPad version (ios-app) and one month later the iPhone/mobile website (web-app). Afterwards were delivered the InApp-purchase of single numbers and the auto-renewable subscriptions for the iPad app. Websites: www.ilfoglio.it / mob.ilfoglio.it
