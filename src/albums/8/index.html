Avanscoperta

Avanscoperta is a consulting and training company focused on software development. They needed a website to promote not only their courses, but mainly the people - and their knowledge - behind these courses. For this reason the only visual elements used in the pages are the teacher's photos and a huge photographic header about "human exploration". The website was built using Wordpress with custom post-types and a custom-made template, in HTML5/CSS3, responsive and multilingual. Website: www.avanscoperta.it
