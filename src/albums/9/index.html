Relais dell'Abbazia

The Relais dell'Abbazia is a beautiful and elegant B&B located in the historical center of Verona. I was contacted to define from scratch their corporate image and their presence on the web. I conceived their logo, designed the business cards, coordinated the photo-shooting of the rooms (with a professional interiors photographer), designed and built their website (in HTML5/CSS3, responsive and multi-lingual). The success of the website as a promotional tool went beyond the most optimistic expectations, revealing itself as a fundamental support in the activity of the B&B. Website: www.relais-abbazia.it
